JUNE EVENTS

June 9 – IPC India Organized webinar on Importance of Design in Electronics Manufacturing and highlighted the global electronics design standards developed for the assembly – interfaces – printed board industry. Thanks to Mr. Amba Prasad (IPC Trainer for Designer Certification Program) for his valuable insight & presentation. Webinar is attended by 100+ Member staffs involved in design participated from India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Philippines, Saudi Arabia & UAE.

June 30 - IPC India Regional Webinar on “Workforce Training Overview” highlighted launch of two new courses for Operator/Newcomers - Electronics Assembly for Operator (EAO) & Wire Harness Assembly Operator (WHO). The Instructor Led courses will be organized through virtual & live mode in the region. IPC India will be connecting with Govt Organizations & Technical Institutes for implementing this course for students in final year of electronics. Skill development is an important driver to address improving employability, productivity and inclusive growth. For the course, Introductory offer with discount to be provided to first 5 Members with nomination of 10+ candidates
